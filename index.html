    Welcome to our Engineering Education Research Centre.

    Our Centre connects people, projects, publications, and research with resources, forums and events in engineering education.

    Our primary goal is to continually improve learning experiences for engineering students. Our vision is to support research projects aimed at developing expertise for curriculum innovation, investigative teaching, and implementing a research-informed, relevant curriculum for future engineers.

    This website serves as a communal hub to support research, events, seminar programmes, postgraduate research, funding opportunities, peer review of teaching, the Teaching Matters Forum, and more.
